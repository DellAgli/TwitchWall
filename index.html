What is Twitch Wall?

Twitch Wall is a platform for automatic curation of multiple Twitch.tv streams. Simply tell us which streamers you are interested in watching and we will automatically fill the page with a wall of content.

How Do I Use This Beautiful Contraption?

Hover over the Twitch logo in the bottom right-hand corner and click the gear to configure the site. Once you've let us know which streams you are interested in, click the twitch logo to reload the page begin watching.
